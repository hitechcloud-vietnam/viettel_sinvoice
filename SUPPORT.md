## Support scope

Commercial support covers installation guidance, configuration review, module defects, compatibility notes, WHMCS hook behavior, and Viettel API troubleshooting with sanitized evidence.

## Out of scope

- Custom development outside the licensed scope.
- Server administration unrelated to the module.
- Viettel account approval, tax authority policy decisions, or third-party outages.
- Debugging with unredacted customer data or credentials.

## Response time

Commercial requests are generally handled within 24-48 business hours depending on severity and support agreement.
