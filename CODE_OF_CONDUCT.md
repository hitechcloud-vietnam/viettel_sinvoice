# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participants must act professionally, respectfully, and constructively when reporting issues, requesting support, or contributing changes.

## Acceptable behavior

- Clear technical communication.
- Respectful disagreement.
- Responsible disclosure of security issues.
- Protection of customer data and credentials.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Public disclosure of vulnerabilities before remediation.
- Sharing secrets, customer data, or production logs without authorization.
- Attempts to bypass licensing or platform security.

## Reporting

Report conduct or security concerns privately to `report@photuesoftware.com`.

## Enforcement

The maintainers may reject support, revoke access, or take legal action for violations.
